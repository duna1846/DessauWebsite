Garrison Linn

Ultrafast Optics and ARPES on Strongly Correlated Materials

Garrison is the fourth of five sons. It was his eldest brother, William, that first introduced him to Quantum Mechanics in the summer before college. This set Garrison on a course to complete a B.S. in physics at Birmingham-Southern College followed by an M.S. in physics at the University of Alabama at Birmingham.
