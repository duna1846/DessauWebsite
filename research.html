Research

Research in the Dessau group may tackle a variety of interesting topics.

Pump Probe ARPES

In pump probe ARPES, we optically excite, or pump, a material to a non-equilibrium state. We then photoemit, or probe, the sample with femtosecond temporal resolution to measure the electronic structure as a function of time.

Organic Superconductors

Our lab recently measured a high-Tc superconducting gap in potassium doped terphenyl crystals.
