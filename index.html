Dessau Research Group

Angle-Resolved Photoemission Spectroscopy (ARPES) of Quantum Materials

We are spectroscopists using a variety of tools for the study of the electronic structure, magnetic structure, and phase transitions of quantum materials such as high temperature superconductors, topological materials, strongly correlated systems, and strong spin-orbit materials. We are users of synchrotron light facilities around the world and have unique ARPES and laser ARPES instrumentation in our home lab.
